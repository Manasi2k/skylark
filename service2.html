Material Fabricationer

We provides a wide range of bar and sheet and other processing. Materials may include medium carbon steel, alloy steel, stainless steel, aluminum, copper, non-alloy steels and sand castings, steel and copper forging and sand casting, aluminum alloy castings and forging and extrusion, Precision dewaxing casting, powder metallurgy and so on.

Lathe Fabrication

The main use is to remove unwanted parts of the material, leaving behind a nicely shaped workpiece.There are a variety of traditional, CNC lathes and automated robotic lathes on the site, including additional vertical and horizontal lathes. Processing size : Min OD: 3.0 mm Min L: 3.0 mm

More Fabrication

Precision Metal Works (PMW) advanced manufacturing includes CNC Punching, Laser Cutting, Stamping, Forming, Welding and other processes to produce Precision Sheet Metal Products such as custom enclosures, cabinets, housings, card cages and components for our OEM customers.
